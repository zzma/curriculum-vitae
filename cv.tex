\section{Research Summary} 

My research applies an empirical lens to understand and improve the security,
performance, and availability of networked systems. My current research falls into 
two categories: 1) improving the transparency and usability of web server identity/authentication, and 2) 
studying trustworthiness as a property of networked communications, building
upon the properties of confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity. \\
